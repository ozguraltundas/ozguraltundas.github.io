PhD Research Projects

Embryogenic Callus Formation & Transformation QTL (Maize)

2024 – Present

QTL mapping of embryogenic callus induction in the B73 × CML216 population with the goal of identifying genomic regions regulating maize transformation efficiency. Ongoing fine-mapping of a chromosome 2 locus associated with totipotency and regeneration.

Genetics · QTL Mapping · Maize Transformation · Tissue Culture

Sorghum Photosynthetic Traits & Physiological Modeling

2024 – Present

High-throughput multi-environment modeling of NPQt, Phi2, SPAD, and related traits to understand the physiological and genetic basis of photosynthesis responses across nitrogen treatments.

Physiology · Trait Modeling · Sorghum · High-Throughput Phenotyping

Maize Ear Phenotyping Pipelines (V1–V3)

2024 – Present

Development of standardized data cleaning, BLUE/BLUP extraction, heritability estimation, and multi-environment analysis pipelines across three versions of the Maize Ear dataset.

R Pipelines · BLUP/BLUE · Heritability · Data Engineering

Industry & Applied Research Projects

Pink-Fruited Hybrid Tomato Biofortification Project (TSWV/TYLCV)

2020 – 2023

Development of pink-fruited hybrid tomato varieties with TSWV and TYLCV resistance, strong yield components, and enhanced iron uptake and retranslocation efficiency. The developed hybrids may serve as donor material for iron biofortification programs.

Tomato Breeding · Biofortification · MAS · Disease Resistance

Universal Positive Control for MAS Workflows (Tomato)

2023

Creation of a standardized molecular positive control containing key resistance genes (Fol1/2/3, Vd/Va, ToMV, Mi, For) to improve the accuracy and reproducibility of tomato marker-assisted selection pipelines.

Molecular Markers · Quality Control · Tomato Genetics

Academic & Early Research Projects

Piriformospora indica for Boron Toxicity Tolerance in Wheat

2022 – 2023

Investigation of the biostimulant potential of Piriformospora indica under boron toxicity stress in wheat, focusing on physiological responses, antioxidant metabolism, and nutrient uptake.

Biostimulants · Wheat · Abiotic Stress · Plant Physiology

PVY/PVX Resistance Screening in Potato Breeding Lines

2019 – 2020

Undergraduate thesis research evaluating potato genotypes for PVY and PVX resistance using PCR-based molecular markers to support development of virus-resistant donor lines.

Molecular Diagnostics · Potato Breeding · PCR Markers
