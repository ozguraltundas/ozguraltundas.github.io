About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Agronomy and Horticulture, University of Nebraska–Lincoln, working in the Schnable Lab. My research focuses on identifying quantitative trait loci (QTLs) associated with yield-related traits in maize.

Research Interests

  • Quantitative genetics and trait dissection
  • QTL mapping and genome-wide association studies (GWAS)
  • Genomic prediction and plant breeding strategies
  • Maize transformation and embryogenic callus induction
