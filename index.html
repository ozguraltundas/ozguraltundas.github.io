Welcome

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln in the Department of Agronomy and Horticulture, specializing in Plant Breeding & Genetics. My research focuses on maize genomics, quantitative genetics, high-throughput phenotyping, and genetic improvement for agronomic traits.

I work in the Schnable Lab, where I focus on multi-environment trait analysis, high-dimensional phenotyping, and genotype–phenotype integration pipelines.
